About Rodents

Rodents are more demanding than one may think, given their solitary nature. They have multiple needs that have to be accounted for.

Common Rodent Pets

  • Guinea Pigs
  • Hamsters
  • Mice

Feeding

Rodents generally eat the same things e.g vegetables, fruits and some nuts. Water helps in digestion.

Grooming

Like cats, rodents groom themselves. Just take them to the vet.

Housing

Rodents just require cages with their feeding and water bowls.
