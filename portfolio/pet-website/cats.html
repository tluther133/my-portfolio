About Cats

Cats, though mostly self-sufficient, won't require as much of your attention as possible.

Feeding

Cats are hunters, so most of the time, they will hunt rodents for themselves. But, to earn it's trust, give it some meat. Remember, mature cats are lactose intolerant!

Grooming

Cats generally groom themselves, so this should not worry you much. Just take it to the vet occasionally.

Housing

Cats are clean creatures, so their environment isn't. Always clean their homes, and their feeding plates to prevent them from leaving.
