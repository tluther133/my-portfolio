Overview

Cottonwood will be having a vast array of activities, most which will involve its main environment: Water. Snorkelling, which will be done on morning-afternoon hours under supervision by a trained personnel, who has experience with the ocean for the past 10 years.

Scuba Diving

Scuba diving is offered on three areas; shallow waters(next to the beach), medium-depth areas, and deep areas, all of which provide the option to go without supervision.

Beachside Sports

Beachside sports will be held, and the staff will be more than willing to join in, and sreve refreshments right after the game!

Jetskiing

Jetskiing, and any other activity involving skiing will be done as long as the sun is up. These activities will be unavailable during the night to prevent accidents.
